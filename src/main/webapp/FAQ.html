What formats are supported on the Apple iPhone and iPad ? Please consult the latest revision of the iPhone user guide for up to date information on supported formats. The following should be supported by your device: Audio - Both the iPhone and the iPad can play audio attachments, such as .mp3, .aac, .wav, and .aiff Documents - Files can be downloaded and viewed in any of the following formats: .doc, .docx, .htm, .html, .key, .numbers, .pages, .pdf, .ppt, .pptx, .rtf, .txt, .vcf, .xls, .xlsx Images - Both support the display of image attachments in many commonly used formats (.jpeg, .gif, and .tiff) inline Video - Correctly encoded video files can be played back in .m4v, .mp4 and .mov formats What formats are supported on Android devices ? Please consult the latest revision of Android supported media formats for up to date information on supported formats. The following should be supported by your device: Audio - Any of .3gp, .aac, .flac, .imy, .m4a, .mid, .mp3, .mp4, .mxmf, .ota, .xmf, .rtttl, .rtx, .wav Images - Any of .bmp, .gif, .jpg, .png and .webp Video - Correctly encoded video files can be played back in .3gp, .mkv, .mp4, .ts or .webm formats
